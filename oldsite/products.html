Vade
Vade is an open world, 3D space-sim. It takes place in an alternate universe where the United States has had a secret deep-space program since the 1960s. The real captain of the Apollo 11, Michael Collins heads a reconnaissance mission into the great blackness of space in a modified VW Kombi.






Project Calendar
Project Calendar is a dynamic life simulator. You begin your life by allocating time to activities and experiences such as exercise, friends, school, etc. These result in changes to your relationships, status and events that may occur. This was originally a text-based title, we added some pretty pictures and BAM! Mouse driven game.
